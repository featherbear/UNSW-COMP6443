## Enumeration

Enumeration is the process of finding sites/domains/locations that may be of interest

* Passive
  * Artifacts
  * Domains with a shared SSL certificate
* Active
  * Brute force
  * dirb
  * dirbuster
  * gobuster
  * subbrute
  * sublist3r
  * amass
  * fierce.pl

## Defense

* Delete content that isn't necessary
* Restrict access to non-hardened content
* Test your applications, fix the bugs
